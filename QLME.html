What is Moby

Mobile Expert QuickLinks

Step 1
Let's get started.

Tap on these Shortcuts in order and install them to your REMO. You may have to connect your REMO to your personal hotspot and temporarily disable the VPN in settings, but note that the device must be restarted after to properly work again.

1. Upfront Costs 2. Monthly Costs 3. System Updates 4. Plans 5. C2 6. PlusUp Calc 7. The HUB 8. Power BI 9. ECS 10. QuickAccess 2 11. Stores 12. Customer Welcome 13. Promotions
Step 2
Putting it together.

QuickLinks is a highly modular system. You can organize things however you want and even add your custom shortcuts into the mix. The following is a guide on how to put things together if you just want a quick and easy setup.

1.

Hold your finger down on an empty space on the REMO home screen until you see a "+" icon appear in the top left corner.

2.

Add Shortcut and Calendar widgets to your home screen like pictured above. You can press and hold on Shortcut widgets to change what QuickLinks are displayed.

Step 3
The small things.

Great things take a little work.

Make sure to press the three little dots on the Customer Welcome QuickLink and set it to open Customer Welcome when pressed. Also keep in mind that the Promotions QuickLink requires you to download the promotions PDF each time it updates using The Hub specifically to the Downloads folder on your REMO as "Promos.pdf" in order to function!

Add the remaining QuickLinks to your REMO.

You can add the rest of the QuickLinks to your REMO by adding more widgets or by doing the following:

Shortcuts App > Press 3 Dots on Desired Shortcut > Press Share Icon > Add to Home Screen

Perfection is only possible with you.

T-Mobile changes things a lot and sometimes things break. If you encounter something not working properly or see any dead links, please message me on slack!
